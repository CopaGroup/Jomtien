About Copacabana Group

A Luxury Destination in Pattaya

With over 20 years of experience in Pattaya's thriving real estate market, Copacabana Group has earned its reputation as a leader in luxury property development.

Our Journey & Milestones:

  • Trusted Agent: Started with land sales and became the sole agent for iconic projects like Riviera Jomtien, Venetian Jomtien, and more.
  • First Flagship Project: Launched Copacabana Beach Jomtien, a breakthrough beachfront development recognized for innovative design and numerous awards.
  • Latest Venture: Currently developing Copacabana Coral Reef Jomtien, promising to be another landmark in our prestigious portfolio.
  • Our Passion: Dedicated to delivering world-class designs, luxury living spaces, and exceptional investment opportunities for our clients.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

"Determination, honesty, and sincerity"

As a leading real estate development company in Pattaya, we are dedicated to creating exceptional accommodations that symbolize success. With unwavering determination, honesty, and sincerity, we strive to provide our customers with the highest quality living experiences.

Our Mission

  • Customer Focus: Create luxurious and comfortable accommodations that exceed customer expectations.
  • Design Excellence: Design spaces that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing.
  • Team Empowerment: Foster a positive work environment and prioritize the development of our personnel.

Social Responsibility

We are committed to contributing positively to society and enhancing the quality of life for our community. Through dedicated initiatives and thoughtful development, we aim to create a thriving and successful environment for everyone.
